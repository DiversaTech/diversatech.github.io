Frequently Asked Questions

How is DiversaTech different from other consulting clubs?

We think differently: while other clubs often focus on major and past experiences when evaluating candidates, we welcome students of all backgrounds to DiversaTech! We look for a versatile, diversity-embracing mindset in our selection process.

Contrary to popular belief, tech companies—at least successful ones—do not only hire only computer science and business majors. DiversaTech emulates this belief that interdisciplinary teams discover the most robust solutions to challenging problems. As practice (in DiversaTech) makes perfect (in your career), join DiversaTech to have a head start!

I have no experience consulting. Does it still make sense for me to apply to DiversaTech?

Your unique background is an asset to our organization! We want to see that you have demonstrated strong interest and passion in your past activities, which do not necessarily need to be tech-related.

Once you join us, we hold a bootcamp series for new members which will bring you up to speed, and you will continue to build skills through your consulting projects. Your decision to apply should be a no-brainer!

What is DiversaTech's consulting project structure?

DiversaTech's project structure consists of 4 project teams, each with 2 project managers and 6-7 consultants. Each team is in charge of addressing the needs of the specific corporate partner they work with.

What does DiversaTech do to ensure success?

DiversaTech recruits some of the brightest, passionate, and hard-working students at Cal. Through an organized timeline and effective leadership, we aim to always deliver maximum value to our clients.
