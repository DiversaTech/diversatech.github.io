Frequently Asked Questions

How is DiversaTech different from other consulting clubs?

close

We welcome students of all majors and experiences to DiversaTech! Contrary to popular belief, tech companies (at least successful ones) do not only hire only computer science + business majors. DiversaTech emulates this belief that interdisciplinary teams discover the most robust solutions to challenging problems. As practice (in DiversaTech) makes perfect (in your career), join DiversaTech to have a head start!

more_vert

I have no experience consulting. Does it make sense for me to apply to DiversaTech?

close

Your unique background is an asset to our organization! We want to see that you have demonstrated strong interest and passion in your past activities (not necessarily tech related). Once you join the club, we hold a bootcamp series which will bring you up to speed. Your decision to apply should be a no-brainer!

more_vert

What is DiversaTech's project structure?

close

DiversaTech's project structure consists of 4 project teams, each with 2 project managers and 6-7 consultants. Each team is in charge of addressing the needs of the specific corporate partner they work with.

more_vert

What does DiversaTech do to ensure success?

close

DiversaTech recruits some of the brightest, passionate, and hard-working students at Cal. Through an organized timeline and effective leadership, we hope to deliver the most value to our clients.
