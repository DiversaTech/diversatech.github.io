About Us



Our goal is to challenge and inspire interdisciplinary students to excel in technology careers and foster a diverse environment for people of all academic backgrounds to collaborate and create unexpected outcomes.

Each semester, we form tech consulting teams and bring together students from various disciplines to solve complex and challenging problems. We are unique from other consulting organizations in that we incorporate engineering with our business recommendations in order to formulate more holistic, creative, and realistic strategies.
