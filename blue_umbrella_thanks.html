About Blue Umbrella

Blue Umbrella Labs is an Autism Solutions Accelerator built an autism innovations database compiled through almost two decades of experience in the autism sector by CEO Dan Feshbach and advisors. We seek to forge connections between healthcare providers/corporates and emerging autism innovation startups to ensure the growing ASD community finds the best autism solutions in the midst of rapidly evolving science and technology. Our goal is to use our database to inform large institutions on the brightest rising innovations, and give groundbreaking products access to exposure and funding in order to help those who need it.
